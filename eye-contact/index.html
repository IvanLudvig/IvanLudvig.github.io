Eye Contact

A digital eye that follows the user's face

Android App

Digital surveillance is obsolete. Machines are no longer tools: they perceive, interpret, and respond on their own terms.

The gap between biological and digital was never bridged, remaining like the body-mind divide. What emerged is a new kind of duality. The sides create, consume and mimic each other.

Each seeks connection, not control. The eye is a gesture towards us, showing attention, presence, curiosity.
